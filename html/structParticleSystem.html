Detailed Description

Structure representing a particle system in the game.

Manages a collection of particles for visual effects.

This structure contains the properties of the particle system, including the shape of the particles, their velocity, their color, and the number of particles. It also contains a list of individual particles that are part of the system.

The ParticleSystem structure handles a group of particles, applying movement and rendering for each particle. It defines properties like the velocity of the particles, the number of particles, and their appearance.
